Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Chicken Teriyaki is a popular Japanese dish consisting of chicken that has been marinated in a special sauce and then grilled or broiled. The sauce is made with soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. It is often served with rice and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 pound chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish
  • Green onions, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, combine soy sauce, mirin, sugar, garlic, and ginger. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Place chicken in a shallow dish and pour half of the sauce over it. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned on both sides and cooked through.
  4. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and water. Add to the remaining sauce and bring to a boil, stirring constantly until thickened.
  5. Serve chicken with sauce and garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
